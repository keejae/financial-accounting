Biography

Philip Keejae Hong is a Professor of Accounting at Central Michigan University. He earned his Ph.D. in Accounting from the University of Illinois and has since devoted his career to teaching and research. Prior to joining academia, he worked as a Financial Reporting Analyst at Citigroup. Professor Hong teaches financial, auditing, and managerial accounting across both undergraduate and graduate programs. His research primarily explores the processing and utilization of accounting information by participants in the capital markets. His findings have been published in journals such as Accounting Horizons, the Journal of Business, Finance and Accounting, the Journal of Accounting, Auditing and Finance, the Financial Review, Advances in Accounting, and The International Journal of Auditing.

약력

홍필립(Philip Keejae Hong) 교수는 센트럴미시간대학교(Central Michigan University) 회계학 교수입니다. 일리노이 대학교(University of Illinois)에서 회계학 박사 학위를 취득한 이후 교육과 연구에 매진해 왔습니다. 학계에 입문하기 전에는 씨티그룹(Citigroup)에서 재무보고 애널리스트(Financial Reporting Analyst)로 근무했습니다. 홍 교수는 학부와 대학원 과정에서 재무회계, 회계감사, 관리회계를 강의하고 있습니다. 그의 연구는 주로 자본시장 참여자들이 회계정보를 어떻게 처리하고 활용하는지를 탐구하며, 연구 성과는 Accounting Horizons, Journal of Business, Finance and Accounting, Journal of Accounting, Auditing and Finance, Financial Review, Advances in Accounting, The International Journal of Auditing 등의 학술지에 게재되었습니다.
